How to make an

Awesome

Cheese

Sandwich

Ingredients

Bread | Chedder cheese | Tomato | Ranch dressing | Black pepper | Oregano

Step 1

Gather all the ingredients.

Step 2

Chop the tomato into slices.

Step 3

Spread ranch dressing on the bread.

Step 4

Sprinkle black pepper and oregano on the bread.

Step 5

Stack the tomatos and cheese on the bottom slice.

Step 6

Slap the top slice on, cut in half and enjoy!
