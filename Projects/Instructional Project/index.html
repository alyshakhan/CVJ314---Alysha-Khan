How to make an

An Awesome Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of chedder cheese
  • Ranch dressing
  • Black pepper
  • Oregano

Step 1

Gather all the ingredients.

Step 2

Chop the tomato into slices.

Step 3

Spread ranch dressing on the slices of bread.

Step 4

Sprinkle black pepper and oregano on both slices.

Step 5

On the bottom slice, stack the tomato and cheese.

Step 6

Slap the top slice on, cut in half and enjoy!
