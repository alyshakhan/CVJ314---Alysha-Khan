How to make an

An Awesome Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of chedder cheese
  • Ranch dressing
  • Black pepper
  • Oregano

Step 1

Gather all the ingredients. Choose whichever bread you prefer.


Step 2

Chop the tomato into slices.


Step 3

Spread ranch dressing on the slices of bread.


Step 4

Sprinkle black pepper and oregano on both slices.


Step 5

On the bottom slice, place the tomato slices, followed by the chedder cheese.


Step 6

Slap the top slice on, cut in half and enjoy!
